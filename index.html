Our Proffesional Owner

Rizka Wahyu

Owner BSL

BSL is a typical Bandung souvenir that combines traditional Bandung flavors with innovation, using quality basic ingredients such as milk. Bolu Susu Lembang was released in December 2017 at Ciwalk Bandung. Rizka and Anggara started promoting Bolu Susu Lembang through word of mouth and the community. Currently, BSL has become one of the must-have souvenirs for visitors to Lembang and Bandung. The Unique Story of Bolu Susu Lembang, Inspiring! - Bolu Susu Lembang December 20, 2022

M.Faizal Chaniago

Owner Siliwangi

Bolu Kukus Siliwangi, which is produced with the aim of preserving the culture of Bumi Pasundan, opened its first outlet at Bogor Station on October 25, 2019. Currently, Bolu Kukus Siliwangi can be found in Bogor, Bandung, and Sukabumi.
